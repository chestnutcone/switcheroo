Switcheroo

Scheduling made easy

What is Switcheroo?

Switcheroo is a web app that solves the tedious task of arranging shifts for employee and shift switching. It is useful for organization with a lot of employees that have the same position.

Problems addressed

Scheduling

Some managers sit down at their desk and spend hours arranging shifts for employees on Excel. This is horribly inefficient. With Switcheroo, scheduling can be done on a pattern basis. Suppose you have a 2 morning shifts, 2 night shifts, 4 rest days schedule for your employees, you can create that scheduling pattern with Switcheroo and assign to users repeatedly. Furthermore, you can assign shifts automatically across multiple users at once.

Shift Switching

When the employee pool grows, it becomes harder to keep track who is open to accept shifts. With Switcheroo, you no longer have to text every co-workers asking if they are free. The algorithm takes care of finding the right match for you. All you have to do is select who you want switch the shift with. The finalizing of shift switch is automatically sent to manager for approval.

Features for managers

    Schedule assignment based on shifts patterns
  • Shifts are the unit block of schedules. Combine different shifts to form a schedule pattern
  • Schedule patterns are in blocks of days
    Scheduling
  • Assignments are done with starting dates
  • Scheduling can also be done automatic! The algorithm will sort out the optimal shift assignment based on the workers needed per day, employees' preferred work days, and seniority
    View employee's schedule
  • Easily view each employee's schedule in calendar
    Employee management
  • One manager is in charge of a group of employees. The shifts and schedule patterns created belong only to this group
  • Each employee only belongs to one group
  • A manager can be an employee as well. This means you can stack your groups and create a whole organization!
    People
  • Each group can contain different types of employees with different units and positions
  • Units and positions are internal to the group. For example, manager A's unit "logistics" is different from manager B's "logistics". Same for positions
  • You can create as many different types of units and positions as requied. Note: shifts switches are only made within employees that belong to same group, unit, and position

Features for employees

    Shift switching
  • If you want to switch shift with someone on a particular day, all you have to do is choose a shift on the calendar, and click swap. The algorithm will find the a list of possible matches. Once you select a shift to swap with, the request will be sent to the receiver. If receiver agrees, the request will be finalized for approval by manager. If manager agrees, the swap will be done automatically.
  • You can switch a range of shifts all at once! No more texting other employees asking if they are free
  • Options to accept more shifts
  • View shift switch approval status
    Vacation days
  • Directly request vacation dates. Vacation dates will be sent to manager for approval
  • Once approved, the shift scheduling will not assign any shift on the vacation day
  • You can request a range of vacation days for approval
  • View vacation approval status
